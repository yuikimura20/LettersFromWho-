the last message

and with that being said, all the letters have been opened and all the memories have been explored. now its my turn to write back. it's time for me to finally pick up the pen. after all, i spent so many years collecting and collecting letters from others but the amount of times I've written back is actually quite not that much. I have so many things to say but where to start? I guess I just have to start somewhere.
