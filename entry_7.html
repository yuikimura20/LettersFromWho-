Out of all the letters I've ever recieved, only one has ever given me advice. This letter stays in my wallet at all times and sometimes I come back to it as a reminder that wherever people are across in the world, they care for you. A small but impactful letter from my high school English teacher, she writes her tips on studying abroad. Although I have recently lost touch with her, the little slip of paper I have is enough. Even in my second year in the city I find myself doing the exact same things she had told me to do.
