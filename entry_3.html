Letters From Who

From: mom ✿, To: yui ୭ৎ

Letters from my mom come in various forms. She would write me notes on the most random places (like...blank sheets of A4 paper) But those notes have more value to me than anything because they come from her. However, I have to say, she is much more of a texter than a writer. She would always create photo albums of of me when I was younger and send them to me through online and that was her way of storing memories.

My mom is someone who i have created the most memories with. I rarely go on vacation or take trips with people but she is the one person i would willingly get myself out of bed and pack my suitecase full for. My favorite place that i have ever visited with her is her hometown hiroshima. One of the first vacation memories i have with her was the summer of 2010 when we visited an island close to mainland hiroshima. The island was home to many cicadas, summer festivals, fisherman and fireworks. I haven't gone back since. Maybe i should one day.
